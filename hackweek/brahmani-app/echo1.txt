To print a texted line we use:\n     $ echo \"Hello World!!\"\n \n To print a value attributed:\n     $ x=10\n     $ echo $x\n  \nTo remove the spaces and print the output:\n     $ echo -e \"Hello \\b World\"\n  \nTo print words each in a line:\n     $ echo -e \"Hello \\nWorld\"\n  \nTo print words with horizontal tab spaces:\n     $ echo -e \"Hello \\t World\"\n \nTo print specific files:\n     $ echo *.extension\n  \n Options used in Echo:\n     -n : Do not output the trailing new line.\n     -e : Enable interpretation of backslash escapes.\n     -E : Disable interpretation of backslash escapes.
